🎯 Simple Equation Builder - User-Friendly Interface

✨ What's New?

I've created a Simple Equation Builder that makes it easy for anyone to input equations without knowing LaTeX!

🚀 Key Features

📝 Step-by-Step Building

  • Left Side - Build the left side of your equation
  • Right Side - Build the right side of your equation
  • Clear visual indicators show which side you're currently editing

🔢 Simple Number Input

  • Click number buttons (0-9) to add numbers
  • Click variable buttons (x, y, z) to add variables
  • Click operator buttons (+, -, *, /) to add operations

📊 Easy Fraction Creation

  • Click "Add Fraction" button
  • Build numerator (top) step by step
  • Build denominator (bottom) step by step
  • See live preview of your fraction

⚡ Quick Patterns

  • One-click buttons for common expressions
  • "Complex Fraction" - adds (x+1)/(x-1)
  • "x²" - adds x^(2)
  • More patterns coming soon!

🎯 How to Use (Step by Step)

Step 1: Choose Input Mode

Click "Simple Builder" (it's the default now!)

Step 2: Build Left Side

Click "Left Side" button, then:

  • Click numbers and variables to build your expression
  • Use "Add Fraction" for fractions
  • Use "Add Power" for exponents

Step 3: Build Right Side

Click "Right Side" button, then repeat the same process

Step 4: Solve

Click "Solve Equation" to get your step-by-step solution!

📋 Example: Building a Complex Fraction

To build: (x+1)/(x-1) = 1/2

  1. Click "Left Side"
  2. Click "Add Fraction"
  3. In numerator: click x, +, 1
  4. Click "Denominator"
  5. In denominator: click x, -, 1
  6. Click "Add Fraction"
  7. Click "Right Side"
  8. Click "Add Fraction"
  9. In numerator: click 1
  10. Click "Denominator"
  11. In denominator: click 2
  12. Click "Add Fraction"
  13. Click "Solve Equation"
